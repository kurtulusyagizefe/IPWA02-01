What can you do here?

  • Explore CO2 Data: Browse the latest emission statistics for countries worldwide, helping you stay informed about environmental impacts.
  • Contribute to the Cause: Registered scientists can log in to add new data or correct inaccuracies, ensuring our database remains up-to-date and accurate.
  • Access Latest Reports: View the most recent emissions data, including the EDGAR 2024 GHG database covering 1970-2023.
